Who Needs Statutory Audit in Bangalore?

Every Private Limited Company and OPC in Bangalore must get a statutory audit done every financial year, regardless of turnover. Additionally, businesses in Bangalore with turnover exceeding ₹1 crore (trading) or ₹50 lakh (professionals) require a tax audit under Section 44AB of the Income Tax Act.
